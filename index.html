About Us

Hoist Capital Partners is a private investment firm focused on partnering with strong management teams to grow exceptional middle-market companies. We bring strategic guidance, operational support, and long-term capital to every business we invest in.

Investment Strategy

We invest in growth-oriented businesses across a variety of sectors. Our flexible capital and hands-on approach allow us to support both organic growth initiatives and strategic acquisitions. We take pride in aligning our interests with those of our partners.

Portfolio

Our portfolio includes a diverse set of businesses across industrials, services, consumer, and healthcare. We focus on companies with strong fundamentals, defensible market positions, and meaningful growth potential.
